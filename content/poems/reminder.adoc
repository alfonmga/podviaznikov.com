= Reminder
Anton Podviaznikov <anton@podviaznikov.com>

Neither a rich person, +
  nor a poor. +

Neither an artist, +
  nor an engineer. +

Neither a mother of a child, +
  nor a single man. +

Neither a senior adult, +
  nor a teenager. +

Neither a party girl, +
  nor a bookworm. +

Neither a people person, +
  nor a hermited introvert. +

Neither a successful business owner, +
  nor a homeless hippie. +

Neither a professor, +
  nor a thug. +

Neither a casanova, +
  nor a virgin. +

Neither a monk, +
  nor a city lover. +

Neither a globe trotter, +
  nor a villager. +    

Neither a President, +
  nor a cleaner.

No one really knows.
